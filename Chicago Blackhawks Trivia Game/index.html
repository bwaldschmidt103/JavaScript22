How to Play

Welcome to the Chicago Blackhawks Trivia Game! Here are the rules:

  • Read the question and select the correct answer from the options provided.
  • Click "Submit Answer" to check if your answer is correct.
  • If your answer is incorrect, a clue will be provided. You have three chances to guess correctly.
  • Points are awarded based on how many attempts it takes to get the correct answer.
    • 5 points for the first correct guess.
    • 3 points for the second correct guess.
    • 1 point for the third correct guess.
  • Click "Next" to move to the next question.
  • Your score will be displayed at the top of the screen.
